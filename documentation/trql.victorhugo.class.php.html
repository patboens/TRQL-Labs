Filename: trql.victorhugo.class.php Purpose: Multi-purpose treatments of the French language. Search of synonyms, of antonymns, flexed forms of lemmas, etc. VictorHugo examines a sentence in search of "patterns" that correspond to a notion expressed in a specific "context" (e.g. %Mercator.city('Brussels')%' ... where 'Mercator' is the context and 'city' is the notion'. VictorHugo renders results in the French language. Such results can then be translated thanks to Polylogos. Author: Pat Y. Boens Company:
