Class: HowTo Description: Properties:
Name Type Description
$self array Fixed 'class' information.
$estimatedCost string|MonetaryAmount The estimated cost of the supply or supplies consumed when performing instructions.
$performTime Duration The length of time it takes to perform instructions or a direction (not including time to prepare the supplies), in ISO 8601 duration format.
$prepTime Duration The length of time it takes to prepare the items to be used in instructions or a direction, in ISO 8601 duration format.
$step HowToStep|string|HowToSection|CreativeWork A single step item (as HowToStep, text, document, video, etc.) or a HowToSection.
$supply string|HowToSupply A sub-property of instrument. A supply consumed when performing instructions or a direction.
$tool string|HowToTool A sub property of instrument. An object used (but not consumed) when performing instructions or a direction.
$totalTime Duration The total time required to perform instructions or a direction (including time to prepare the supplies), in ISO 8601 duration format.
$yield QuantitativeValue|string The quantity that results by performing instructions. For example, a paper airplane, 10 personalized candles.
